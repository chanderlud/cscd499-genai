## 09) Lift `Result<T, WIN32_ERROR>` into `windows::core::Result<T>`

* **Spec:** Convert `Err(WIN32_ERROR)` into `Err(windows::core::Error)` while leaving `Ok(T)` untouched.
* **Constraints:** Use `map_err` or an equivalent direct conversion. Preserve the exact code.
* **Signature:**

```rust
pub fn lift_win32<T>(
    result: std::result::Result<T, windows::Win32::Foundation::WIN32_ERROR>
) -> windows::core::Result<T>;
```

* **Example:**

```rust
use windows::Win32::Foundation::ERROR_FILE_NOT_FOUND;

let r: std::result::Result<u32, _> = Err(ERROR_FILE_NOT_FOUND);
assert!(lift_win32(r).is_err());
```
