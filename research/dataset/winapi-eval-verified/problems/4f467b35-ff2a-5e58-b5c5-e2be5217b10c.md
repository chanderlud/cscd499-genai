## 02) Convert `WIN32_ERROR` to `HRESULT`

* **Spec:** Convert a `WIN32_ERROR` into its canonical `HRESULT`.
* **Constraints:** Use `HRESULT::from_win32`; do not manually assemble bit patterns.
* **Signature:**

```rust
pub fn win32_to_hresult(code: windows::Win32::Foundation::WIN32_ERROR) -> windows::core::HRESULT;
```

* **Example:**

```rust
use windows::core::HRESULT;
use windows::Win32::Foundation::ERROR_ACCESS_DENIED;

assert_eq!(
    win32_to_hresult(ERROR_ACCESS_DENIED),
    HRESULT::from_win32(ERROR_ACCESS_DENIED.0)
);
```
