06) Atomic replace (ReplaceFileW)

* **Spec:** Replace `target` with `source` atomically (optionally keep backup).
* **Constraints:** Same volume; handle ACL/metadata; careful with open handles.
* **Signature:**

```rust
pub fn atomic_replace(target: &Path, source: &Path, backup: Option<&Path>) -> Result<()>;
```

* **Example:**

```rust
atomic_replace(Path::new("a.txt"), Path::new("a.new"), Some(Path::new("a.bak")))?;
```
