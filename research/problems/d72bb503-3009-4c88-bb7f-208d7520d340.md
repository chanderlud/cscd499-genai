14) Move/Rename with flags (MoveFileExW)

* **Spec:** Rename or move across directories with flags (replace, write-through).
* **Constraints:** Cross-volume move semantics differ; use flags intentionally.
* **Signature:**

```rust
pub fn move_with_flags(src: &Path, dst: &Path, flags: u32) -> Result<()>;
```

* **Example:**

```rust
move_with_flags(Path::new("a.txt"), Path::new("b.txt"), MOVEFILE_REPLACE_EXISTING)?;
```
